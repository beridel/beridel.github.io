About

William Frank is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Earth, Atmospheric and Planetary Sciences at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. His research group focuses on deformation within the solid Earth, whether it’s being shaken by earthquakes or slowly sheared by slow slip.
